Work

Digital Naturalism Laboratories

Director, Co-Founder

2017 - 2019

Dinalab is the greatest test of the research I led as a PhD Student and Professor. We set up our own institution to help develop and evaluate technology in an intimate proximity to nature. Dinalab is a 360m² field station makerspace surrounded by tropical jungle serving as a rapid prototyping workshop for interaction design and field biology. It functions as a lab and residency for visiting artists, scientists, and technologists from around the world.
